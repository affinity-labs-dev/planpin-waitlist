Good to know

How does the free trial work?

Pro starts with a 7 day free trial. You will not be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before then at no cost.

What is a credit?

A credit is one action in PlanPin - import a batch of spots from a reel, TikTok, Google or your notes, or generate a full day-by-day itinerary. Pro gives you 10 credits a week; Max is unlimited.

Pro or Max?

Pro (10 credits a week) is plenty for planning a trip or two at a time. Max removes the weekly limit - best if you are always importing and planning. Max includes everything in Pro.

What happens after I pay on the web?

You will get a one tap link to open PlanPin on your phone with everything already unlocked. The link also lands in your inbox, so you can open it whenever you install the app.

What does it renew at?

After any trial, your plan renews at the price shown above until you cancel. No hidden increases, and you can cancel anytime from your account.

Can I use it on iPhone and Android?

Yes. Your subscription works across iOS and Android with a single account, so your saved spots and trips stay in sync.
